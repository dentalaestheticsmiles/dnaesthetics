Pediatric Dental Care

Gentle, Fun, and Caring Dental Experience for Your Little Ones

Creating Healthy Smiles from an Early Age

At DNA Clinic, we understand that children need special care and attention. Our pediatric dental services are designed to make dental visits a positive, stress-free experience for both children and parents. We create a welcoming, child-friendly environment where your little ones can feel comfortable and safe.

Child-Friendly Environment

Colorful, welcoming spaces designed to put children at ease

Gentle Approach

Experienced pediatric specialists who understand children's needs

Preventive Care

Early intervention to prevent future dental problems

First Dental Visit

Comprehensive examination and gentle introduction to dental care for toddlers and young children

  • Age-appropriate oral health assessment
  • Parent education on oral hygiene
  • Preventive care recommendations

Teeth Cleaning & Fluoride

Professional cleaning sessions designed to be fun and educational, with fluoride treatments to strengthen young teeth

  • Gentle professional cleaning
  • Fluoride application
  • Plaque removal techniques

Dental Sealants

Protective sealants applied to prevent cavities in hard-to-clean areas of children's teeth

  • Painless application process
  • Long-lasting protection
  • Cavity prevention

Early Orthodontic Evaluation

  • Growth and development monitoring
  • Early intervention options
  • Custom treatment plans

Pediatric Fillings

  • Minimal discomfort
  • Aesthetic results

Emergency Pediatric Care
